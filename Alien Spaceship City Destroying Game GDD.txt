Idea: Cartoonish game where you pilot an alien spaceship that is slowely 
decending onto a city where you have to level the buildings with your
laser weapons or die crashing into a building.

Goal: Destroy as many buildings as possible and safely land.

Space: Human city with many buildings of different heights.

Components: Spaceship, buildings, laser weapons.

Mechanics: Shoot, aim, change charge of weapons, switch directions.

Rules: No control of the decending spaceship except the weapons.
Weapons controlled using arrow keys.
Must level enough buildings to land.
Can switch directions prematurely using space but decends 1 level.
